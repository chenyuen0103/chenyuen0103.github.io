About Me

Hello, My name is Yuen Chen, and I am a CS PhD student at University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, advised by Prof. Hari Sundaram and Prof. Han Zhao. Before starting my PhD, I interned at the Max Planck Institute in Tübingen, Germany, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Bernhard Schölkopf and Zhijing Jin. I graduated from UC Berkeley with B.A. in applied mathematics and statistic in December 2022.
